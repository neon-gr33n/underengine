# Party LV

## `LV_get_exp_needed(LV)` → *number*
Fetches the exp needed to level up

| Parameter | Datatype  | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
|`LV` |number |Current level |

**Returns:** EXP needed for next level, -1 for invalid level

## `member_update_stats(member)` → `undefined`
Updates stats depending on level

| Parameter | Datatype  | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
|`member` |object |Member object to update |























## `party_update_stats()` → `undefined`
